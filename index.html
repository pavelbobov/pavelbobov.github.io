SOLUTIONS

Measurement of physical and chemical characteristics of water and air in the requested areas of interest.

Continuous monitoring of water quality in a certain area.

Custom search and monitoring missions.

TECHNOLOGY

Autonomous solar and wind powered boats.

Sensors for measuring chemical and physical characteristics of air and water, sonars, photo and video cameras.

Mission planing, data collection, storing and processing systems.

ABOUT

Our mission is collection of environmental information for land use, conservation, and restoration projects using autonomous vehicles powered by renewable energy. Our current efforts are focused on research and development of solar and wind powered autonomous boats for water quality monitoring.
